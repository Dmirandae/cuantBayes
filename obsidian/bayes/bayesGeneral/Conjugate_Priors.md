

Title: Conjugate Priors
Content: Conjugacy: posterior distribution stays in same family as prior. Makes updating mathematically simple, often just updating parameters. Allows sequential updating. Common pairs: Beta-Binomial, Gamma-Poisson, Normal-Normal, Dirichlet-Multinomial.
