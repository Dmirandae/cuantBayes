

Title: Example: Normal-Normal Conjugacy
Content: Normal is conjugate prior for Normal mean. Prior: θ ~ N(μ₀,τ₀²). Data: Y|θ ~ N(θ,σ²). Posterior: θ|Y ~ N(μ_post,τ_post²). Posterior mean is weighted average of prior mean and data.
