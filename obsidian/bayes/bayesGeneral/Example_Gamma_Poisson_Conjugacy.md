

Title: Example: Gamma-Poisson Conjugacy
Content: Gamma is conjugate prior for Poisson. Prior: λ ~ Gamma(r₀,b₀). Data: y arrivals in time t. Posterior: Gamma(r₀+y, b₀+t). Parameters r₀,b₀ as prior arrivals and total time.
