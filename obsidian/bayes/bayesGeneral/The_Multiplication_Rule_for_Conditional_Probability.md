

Title: The Multiplication Rule for Conditional Probability
Content: P(A∩B) = P(B)P(A|B) = P(A)P(B|A). For n events: P(A₁∩...∩Aₙ) = P(A₁)P(A₂|A₁)P(A₃|A₁∩A₂)...P(Aₙ|A₁∩...∩Aₙ₋₁). Useful for sequential experiments.
