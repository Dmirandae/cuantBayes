

Title: Continuous Distributions: Uniform
Content: Uniform(a,b): completely random number between a and b. PDF constant: f(x) = 1/(b-a) for a≤x≤b. CDF: F(x) = (x-a)/(b-a). Parameters: endpoints a and b.
