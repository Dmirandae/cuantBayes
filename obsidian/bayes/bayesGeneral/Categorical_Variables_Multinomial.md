

Title: Categorical Variables: Multinomial
Content: Multinomial(n,p): generalization of Binomial for trials with multiple categories. PMF: P(X₁=x₁,...,Xₖ=xₖ) = [n!/(x₁!...xₖ!)] p₁^{x₁}...pₖ^{xₖ}. Tracks objects in k categories.
