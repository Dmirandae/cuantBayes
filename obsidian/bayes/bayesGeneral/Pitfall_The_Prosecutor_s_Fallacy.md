

Title: Pitfall: The Prosecutor's Fallacy
Content: Confusing P(Evidence|Innocence) with P(Innocence|Evidence). Even if P(E|I) is very small, P(I|E) might be large if P(I) is large. Example: P(I)=0.999, P(E|I)=0.001 → P(I|E)≈0.526.
