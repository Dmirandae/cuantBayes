

Title: Continuous Distributions: Normal
Content: Normal(μ,σ²): bell-shaped curve central to Central Limit Theorem. PDF: f(x) = [1/√(2πσ²)] e^{-(x-μ)²/(2σ²)}. Models additive effect of many small factors.
