

Title: Bayes' Theorem
Content: Posterior ∝ Likelihood × Prior. Formula: Pr(θ|D) = [Pr(D|θ) × Pr(θ)] / Pr(D). Posterior is updated plausibility; Likelihood counts ways data could be produced; Prior is initial plausibility; Evidence normalizes.
