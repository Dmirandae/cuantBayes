

Title: Example: Beta-Binomial Conjugacy
Content: Beta is conjugate prior for Binomial. Prior: p ~ Beta(a,b). Data: k successes in n trials. Posterior: Beta(a+k, b+n-k). Parameters a,b as prior counts of successes/failures.
